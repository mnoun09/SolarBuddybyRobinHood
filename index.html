Solar Buddy
by Robin Hood

Designed to Combat Hypothermia

The Solar Buddy is a solar-powered backpack which includes a heated sleeping bag. Solar panels on the outside of the bag charges the heated sleeping bag and anything else the consumer may want to power.

The Homeless Problem in LA

The image shows a homeless encampment at 1st and Broadway in LA

Homelessness is a worldwide issue, however it is especially prominent in a metropolitan area such as LA. Rising rent and the lack of laws protecting tenants are leading to increase homelessness in Los Angeles.
